🎯 ¿Qué es este curso?

Este NO es un curso de inglés típico. Es un programa práctico e intensivo diseñado específicamente para abogados y profesionales del derecho que necesitan entender, leer, redactar y hablar inglés jurídico en contextos reales.

✅ Lo que aprenderás:

⚖️ Análisis de Casos Reales

Comprender y analizar opiniones reales de las Cortes Supremas de EE.UU. y Reino Unido (casos 2023-2024)

📝 Redacción de Contratos

Redactar cláusulas contractuales en inglés (NDAs, contratos laborales, acuerdos de licencia)

🏛️ Sistemas Judiciales

Navegar el sistema judicial federal estadounidense y la jerarquía judicial británica con confianza

💼 Comunicación Legal

Comunicarte efectivamente en inglés jurídico (emails, mociones, memorandos)

🌍 Derecho Comparado

Comparar sistemas: Common law vs. Civil law, UK vs. US employment law, EU competition law

🌐 Práctica Internacional

Trabajar con clientes internacionales y comprender asuntos legales transfronterizos

📚 Programa del Curso - 15 Módulos

📚 MODULE 1: US & UK Court Systems

Understanding Anglo-American Judicial Institutions

  • Federal vs. state courts, Supreme Court structure
  • Court of Appeal, Crown Court vs. Magistrates' Court
  • How cases move through the system
  • Reading court opinions and judicial terminology
  • Legal vocabulary in context

📚 MODULE 2: Contract Law Fundamentals

Reading and Understanding Contracts in English

  • Offer, acceptance, consideration
  • Force majeure clauses, termination provisions
  • How contracts are structured in common law
  • Case Study: MUR Shipping BV v. RTI Ltd [2024] UKSC 18
  • Contract vocabulary and drafting style

📚 MODULE 3: Employment Law - UK vs. US

Comparing Employment Systems

  • At-will employment concept (US)
  • Unfair dismissal protection (UK)
  • Discrimination law, EEOC function
  • Employment tribunals and redundancy
  • Reading tribunal decisions

📚 MODULE 4: Company Formation

Understanding Corporate Structures

  • Private Limited Company (Ltd), LLC, PLC
  • How companies are formed
  • Required documents and procedures
  • Differences between UK and US systems
  • Corporate documentation vocabulary

📚 MODULE 5: Corporate Governance

Directors' Duties and Corporate Structure

  • Board of directors role
  • Shareholders' rights and fiduciary duties
  • Derivative actions
  • Companies Act 2006 key provisions
  • Reading governance documents

📚 MODULE 6: Securities & Capital Markets

Understanding Financial Regulation

  • Securities Act 1933, Securities Exchange Act 1934
  • FCA regulations, IPO process
  • Disclosure requirements, SEC enforcement
  • Case Study: SEC v. Jarkesy (2024)
  • Financial legal terminology

📚 MODULE 7: Patents & Trademarks

Intellectual Property Protection

  • Patent eligibility criteria
  • Trademark registration process
  • Opposition proceedings, Madrid Protocol
  • Differences: USPTO, UKIPO, EPO
  • Reading patent claims and IP documentation

📚 MODULE 8: Copyright & Trade Secrets

IP Rights and Protection

  • Copyright Act 1976 basics, DMCA provisions
  • Fair use vs. fair dealing differences
  • Defend Trade Secrets Act
  • Licensing concepts and infringement cases
  • IP litigation language

📚 MODULE 9: Civil Procedure

How Civil Cases Work

  • Pleadings structure
  • Discovery/disclosure concept
  • Summary judgment, trial procedure
  • Federal Rules vs. UK CPR
  • Reading procedural documents and motions

📚 MODULE 10: Constitutional Law

Landmark Supreme Court Cases

  • Separation of powers, judicial review
  • Presidential immunity doctrine
  • Administrative law principles
  • Case Studies: Trump v. United States (2024), Loper Bright (2024)
  • Briefing cases and constitutional reasoning

📚 MODULE 11: Administrative Law

Agency Powers and Limits

  • Administrative Procedure Act
  • Agency rulemaking and judicial review
  • What Chevron deference was
  • Case Study: Loper Bright (2024) - overturning Chevron
  • Reading administrative decisions

📚 MODULE 12: EU Law Fundamentals

European Union Legal System

  • CJEU role and structure, European Commission
  • Four freedoms, direct effect doctrine
  • Supremacy doctrine, preliminary reference
  • Case Studies: Illumina/Grail (2024), climate litigation
  • EU legal terminology

📚 MODULE 13: Competition Law / Antitrust

Market Regulation

  • Sherman Act, Clayton Act
  • TFEU Articles 101-102
  • Merger control, cartel enforcement
  • Abuse of dominance concept
  • Case Study: Intel v. European Commission (2024)

📚 MODULE 14: Human Rights Law

ECHR and International Standards

  • European Convention on Human Rights
  • ECtHR procedure, key articles (2, 3, 6, 8, 10)
  • Admissibility requirements
  • Margin of appreciation doctrine
  • Case Study: Verein KlimaSeniorinnen v. Switzerland (2024)

📚 MODULE 15: Criminal Law & Procedure

Anglo-American Criminal Justice

  • US criminal procedure (4th, 5th, 6th Amendments)
  • Miranda rights, plea bargaining
  • UK criminal procedure (PACE 1984)
  • Crown Prosecution Service
  • Case Study: United States v. Rahimi (2024)

🎯 Habilidades de Lenguaje Integradas

Legal Writing & Reading Comprehension está integrada en TODOS los módulos:

  • ✅ Reading: Court opinions, contracts, statutes, regulations, legal correspondence
  • ✅ Writing: Case briefs, legal analysis, comparative summaries
  • ✅ Vocabulary: Legal terminology, Latin phrases, standard expressions
  • ✅ Analysis: Understanding holding vs. dicta, ratio decidendi, legal reasoning
